ADHD

Note: Symptoms vary for everyone and this isn’t a diagnosis.

Common Symptoms

  • Unable to stay focused or organized
  • Restlessness or impulsivity
  • Hyperfocusing on specific tasks
  • Forgetfulness and losing things

Additional Symptoms

  • Difficulty following instructions
  • Easily distracted by surroundings
  • Difficulty managing time
  • Frequent mood swings

Coping Strategies

  • Try doing things that help you relax
  • Get enough quality sleep
  • Take breaks when you feel overwhelmed
  • Go on walks to ease your mind

More Ways to Cope

  • Do puzzles or coloring therapy
  • Start a small collection for focus
  • Talk to a trusted friend or family member
  • Break tasks into smaller steps
