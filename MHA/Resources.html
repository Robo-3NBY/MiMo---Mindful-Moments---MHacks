Resources

        

It's always okay to need help! If you or someone you know needs it, here's some places to start:

        
              
  • 998: Suicide & Crisis Hotline - immediate + long-term support (NOT for emergencies)
    •           
  • 833-789-8713: Mental Health Hotline - Free and confidential conversations, available 24/7
  • 1-800-950-NAMI: National Alliance on Mental Illness - Provides info on mental conditions and how to deal with them
  • Text HOME to 741741 - National Text line for anyone in crisis, 24/7
