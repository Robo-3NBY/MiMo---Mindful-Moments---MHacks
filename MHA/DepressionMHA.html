Depression

Note: Symptoms vary for everyone and this isn’t a diagnosis.

Common Symptoms

  • Persistent sadness
  • Loss of interest in activities
  • Fatigue or lack of energy
  • Irritability or mood swings

Additional Symptoms

  • Sleep disturbances
  • Changes in appetite or weight
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Feelings of hopelessness or guilt

Coping Strategies

  • Go on short walks outside
  • Reconnect with your passions
  • Give yourself breaks when struggling
  • Spend some time alone to recharge

More Helpful Tips

  • Meditate or practice deep breathing
  • Watch comforting shows or movies
  • Write down your thoughts and emotions
  • Talk to a trusted friend or family member
